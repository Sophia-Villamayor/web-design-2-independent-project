Get to Know Us!

Strings Attached Music Academy is devoted to teach and help pupils learn to play new instruments, no matter the skill level! We’re here to unlock new levels of creativity and find your sound.

How you'll find your sound

Whether you’re as loud as a trombone, or graceful as a flute. These workshops will allow you to be able try new instruments, and learn from professionals!

No Music, No Problem.

Strings Attached has you covered for all instruments and music sheets! Although we do incourage musicians to bring their own instruments that they may already own, we have plenty to lend for those who do not have their own.

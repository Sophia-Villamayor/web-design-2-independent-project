Find the Right One for You!

See which instrument description matches your personality, and choose the right one to play with!

Trombone

A brass family instrument that is perfect for those who like to be heard but from a distance. They’re the missing puzzle piece to any ensemble!

Alto Saxophone

If you like to be heard and carry the melody, then the Alto Saxophone is for you! You’ll be known as the main character in your own movie.

Tenor Saxophone

If you like to go with the flow and have your voice heard from the side, then the Tenor Saxophone is for you!

Bari Saxophone

Just like the trombone, you can be heard from the sideline and still make a huge impact.

Trumpet

A brass family instrument that is mostly used in jazz music. They are perfect for those who love to be loud and add a little spice to music.

Flute

You make everything graceful and carry a shy and light personality, your voice may be soft, but it will be heard!

Clarinet

You’re the backbone to everything, you have a shy voice but not afraid to make yourself known! You’re definitely competing with the Alto Saxophones, but you stand out in your own way.

Drums

You carry the beat to everything, you take control of yourself and inspire those who follow.

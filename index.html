Get to Know Us!

Strings Attached Music Academy is devoted to teach and help pupils learn to play new instruments, no matter the skill level! We’re here to unlock new levels of creativity and find your sound.

How you'll find your sound

Whether you’re as loud as a trombone, or graceful as a flute. These workshops will allow you to be able try new instruments, and learn from professionals! You have choices from Saxophones, Clarinets, Trumpets, Trombones, Flutes, Drums to play! For those who are more musically inclined, there is even a workshop to learn how to conduct!
